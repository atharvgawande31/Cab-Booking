Our Story & Mission

Founded in **2015**, **RentACar** was created with a simple mission: to provide transparent, safe, and easily accessible car rentals across all major Indian metropolitan areas. We understand that travel in India demands reliability, flexibility, and **trust**.

We started with a small fleet of reliable sedans in Mumbai and have since expanded to over **25 cities**, featuring everything from economic hatchbacks to luxury 7-seater SUVs. Our commitment remains the same: to **put the driver first**.
