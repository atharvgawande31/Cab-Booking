1.2 Rental Conditions

  • **Driver Requirements:** All drivers must be over 21 years of age and hold a valid driver's license for a minimum of one year.
  • **Vehicle Use:** Vehicles must be returned to the agreed drop-off location and time. Late returns may incur hourly or daily charges.

All prices are listed in Indian Rupees (₹). We require a security deposit upon pickup, which will be refunded upon the safe return of the vehicle. All cancellations made less than 24 hours before pickup are subject to a cancellation fee equal to one day's rental rate.

*Effective Date: October 2, 2025*

2.1 Information We Collect

We collect personal information necessary for providing our rental services, including:

Your data is used solely to process bookings, verify driver eligibility, process payments, and improve our services. We **do not sell** your personal data to third parties.
